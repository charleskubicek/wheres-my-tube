    <p>
      <a href="http://www.jibble.org/">Someone</a> thought it would be a good idea to see what the London Underground map would look
      like if it was superimposed on a Google map. Seeing as I already had a list of latitudes
      and longitudes for all of the stations on the Tube, I was able to fairly quickly put
      together this map. It's not perfect - where more than one line runs parallel with each
      other the lines are drawn on top of each other, and track between stations is only shown
      as a straight line between the two stations.
