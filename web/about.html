WheresMyTube.com

A "mash-up" between Google Maps and the TFL London Underground Arrival boards. The Arrival boards are scanned and the resuls translated in to Map coordinates and plotted on the map.

Extreme care is taken to minimise the amount of requests made to the TFL Web site. Train location are only ever updated once per minute and the results cached and stored for future requests. A number of optimizing algorithms also enusre the minial amount of requests are ever made, and no requests are made if no users are on the site.
